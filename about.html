Our Story

Vitale Clothing started three years ago when I decided it was time for a change. The change had to do with a dream of putting my love of art and design into something with a voice, with a message. Vitale Clothing is that dream, it’s that voice. I want my clothing line to shout a message that inspires others to pursue their dreams and goals. I want it to encourage people to not give up when times are hard, but to press on. Persistence, diligence, and inspiration can make a difference in your life and in the lives of people everywhere. Don’t stop dreaming, start believing. Most importantly: Don’t be an island; make a difference.
